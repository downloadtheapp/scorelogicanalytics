Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to our most common contact and support inquiries.

How fast can I expect a reply?

For standard inquiries via our contact form or email, we aim to respond within 24 hours. Enterprise clients with SLA agreements receive priority routing and dedicated account manager support.

Do you offer in-person consultations?

Yes. While most of our demonstrations and platform onboarding take place virtually, professional sports organizations can arrange in-person meetings at our Mumbai office or via site visits upon request.

Members of the media looking for quotes, platform screenshots, or data insights for sports journalism can contact our media team directly at contact@scorelogicanalytics.com with the subject line "Media Request".
