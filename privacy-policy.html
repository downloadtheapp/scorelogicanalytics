Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ScoreLogic Analytics. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to:

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law in India.
